AC to DC converter with voltage regulator - At the very first we connected a step down transformer which converts ~250 voltage to 18 voltage (AC) , then connects a bridge rectifier (using diodes 1N4007) which converts AC to DC with a capacitor (4700 uf 25 voltage) to stabilize the DC voltage, after this we connect a Red LED (with 1 Kohm .25 Watt) to identify the above circuit works good. After the red LED we connect a Potentiometer (1 Kohm) & a mosfade (100Kohm IRFZ44N) to regulate the voltage and this place you also connect the display to observe the live voltage. The negative side of the electrode used in voltameter represents the cathode , and the opposite electrode represents the anode Old Website
